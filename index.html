Botelle

About

Botelle is an app that allows people who live in a communal setting, such as dorms and apartments, to have a shared grocery shopping list. By having a shared grocery list, it allows a single person from the community to go to the grocery store. Botelle is designed to have one person go for a group of people, it allows people to save time and money.
